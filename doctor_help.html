Q1. I want report of sample_id 2345

Q2. I want report of sample_id from 2345 to 2350

Q3. I want report of MRD No SUR/12/0000123

Q4. I want report of 'Maganbhai' admitted in Medicine Unit-6

Q5. Report says that "Sample ID XXXX is not verified(Call the lab)"

Tick mark only those boxes which you wish to include in search.

A1. I want report of sample_id 2345

	write "2345" in [from_sample_id] box. 
	tickmark its checkbox(blue,left side). 
	click [print]

A2. I want report of sample_id from 2345 to 2350

	write "2345" in [from_sample_id] box. 
	tickmark its checkbox(blue,left side). 
	
	write "2350" in [to_sample_id] box. 
	tickmark its checkbox(blue,left side).
	 	
	click [print]

A3. I want report of MRD No SUR/12/0000123

	write "SUR/12/0000123" in [patient_id] box. 
	tickmark its checkbox
	
	click [print]

A4. I want report of 'Maganbhai' admitted in Medicine Unit-6

	write "Magan%" in [patient_name] box. 
	tickmark its checkbox(blue,left side). 
	
	select "Medicine" in [clinician] box. 
	tickmark its checkbox
	
	select "6" in [unit] box. 
	tickmark its checkbox	
	click [print]
	
	The reports of "Magan", "Maganbhai", "Maganlal", "Maganbhai Desai" will be shown.
	note use of % to include all choices.

A5. Report says that "Sample ID XXXX is not verified(Call the lab)"

	The report may have failed verification process. 
	It is best to get such report-print from the lab
